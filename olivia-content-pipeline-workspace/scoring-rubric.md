# Olivia Content Pipeline — Scoring Rubric

Derived from statistical analysis of 68 shorts with real YouTube performance data.
Pearson correlation between total score and new viewers: **r = 0.713**

## How To Score

Each short produced by the skill gets scored on the dimensions below.
Score each dimension for both the **first sentence (hook)** and the **full script block**.
Final score = sum of all weighted points.

**Maximum possible score: 30 points per short.**

---

## Tier 1 — High-Impact Dimensions (correlation > 0.5)

### 1. Curiosity Gap (weight: x1.5)
*How much does the viewer NEED to know what happens next?*

| Score | First Sentence | Block |
|-------|---------------|-------|
| 1 | States a fact or tip. No open loop. | Content is informational but self-contained. |
| 2 | Implies something happened but the question is vague. | Some tension but it fades or gets answered too early. |
| 3 | Drops the viewer into unresolved tension — they MUST know the outcome. | Tension escalates through the block; resolution comes only at the end. |

**First Sentence max: 4.5 pts (score × 1.5) · Block max: 4.5 pts (score × 1.5)**

### 2. Emotional Promise (weight: x1)
*Does the viewer feel something — fear, excitement, empathy, recognition?*

| Score | First Sentence | Block |
|-------|---------------|-------|
| 1 | Neutral/informational tone. No feeling evoked. | Block reads like instructions or advice. |
| 2 | Some emotional content but indirect or mild. | Emotional moments exist but aren't sustained. |
| 3 | Immediately evokes a strong emotion (vulnerability, defiance, longing). | Emotional arc builds — viewer goes on a journey. |

**First Sentence max: 3 pts · Block max: 3 pts**

### 3. Informational Promise — Block only (weight: x1)
*Does the block deliver a concrete insight, tactic, or revelation?*

| Score | Block |
|-------|-------|
| 1 | Generic advice. Nothing specific or actionable. |
| 2 | Some useful info but surface-level. |
| 3 | Specific, surprising, or counterintuitive insight the viewer can use. |

**Block max: 3 pts**

---

## Tier 2 — Medium-Impact Dimensions (correlation 0.3–0.5)

### 4. Stakes (weight: binary 2 pts)
*Is something personally at risk — a career, a belief, a relationship, a reputation?*

- **Y (2 pts)**: Something concrete is on the line. Failure, rejection, a decision that could go wrong.
- **N (0 pts)**: Purely instructional or tactical. No personal risk.

**Shorts with stakes average 2x the views of shorts without.**

**First Sentence: 2 pts · Block: 2 pts**

### 5. Specificity (weight: x0.75)
*Are there real names, numbers, places, or concrete details?*

| Score | First Sentence | Block |
|-------|---------------|-------|
| 1 | Abstract, generic. Could apply to anyone. | Vague advice, no anchoring details. |
| 2 | One specific detail (a name, a number, a place). | Some specifics mixed with general statements. |
| 3 | Multiple concrete details. You can picture the scene. | Rich in specifics — names, numbers, moments. |

**First Sentence max: 2.25 pts (score × 0.75) · Block max: 2.25 pts (score × 0.75)**

---

## Tier 3 — Hook Type Bonus

| Hook Type | Bonus |
|-----------|-------|
| Mid-scene drop | +2 pts |
| Bold claim / Hypothetical / Question | +1 pt |
| Setup | 0 pts |
| Series intro | -1 pt |
